About Us

According to the FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization) report 2019, over 20 crore Indians go to sleep empty-stomach every day,
which is 14.5% of the total population of our country. We believe that a single drop of water has the power to create a ripple in an ocean.
Our project ‘Eatero-Meter’ is aimed towards solving food wastage at our campus eateries by helping the clients know the exact count
of heads present at the campus on the same day. This will allow the eateries to know the exact quantity of food required, which will
lower their per day cost of material. While making sure that no one goes home empty stomach.
